About Tsukasa

Tsukasa is a Discord moderation bot with many commands that will allow you to have full control of your server. It can mute people who are spamming, ban people who are being disrespectful and ban people who don't follow the rules. You can fully customize the duration of bans and mutes and even give a reason that will be sent to the user before they are punished. What are you waiting for?

moderation

Tsukasa can help you punish people who misbehave. You can silence them for a period of time or simply ban them directly. Tsukasa uses slash commands that will allow you to easily and effortlessly punish users.

Profiles

You can view the custom profiles of the members that correspond to that server and view their previous profiles. punishments. The longer you stay on a server, the higher your level will be....

Setup System

Our bot has many setup commands, this is one of them. the many stup commands if you want to see them all you can run the command /help.

Roleplay

With more than 10 commands and 100 gifs ranging from hugs, caresses, bites, etc. among others, Tsukasa is the bot with the most variety of roleplay commands... Tsukasa has counters so that you can see how many times you have received hugs, kisses, caresses and more.
